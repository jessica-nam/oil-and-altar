The photographer

Bren shoots the way painters used to pray.

Working almost entirely in available light and candlelight, Bren builds photographs the way an altarpiece is built — slowly, in layers, toward a single point of illumination. The work borrows its palette from oil painting: umber grounds, gilded highlights, oxblood shadows.

Prints are editioned in small runs and finished by hand. Commissions are open for portraiture, editorial, and album artwork.
